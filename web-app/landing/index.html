Without a tracker

  • Spreadsheets emailed between five offices
  • Manual counts take 2+ hours per site
  • SAN stickers transcribed by hand — errors
  • No visibility into low-stock until it's too late
  • New hires can't find which room has G10 docks

With EUC Asset Tracker

  • One shared PWA — install on any phone
  • Point camera at SAN label, OCR reads it instantly
  • Generate Code 128 + QR barcodes from any SAN
  • Low-stock alerts fire automatically per location
  • AI counts boxes in seconds from a single photo

Everything you need to manage EUC inventory

Browser-native ML means no cloud dependencies — scan, count, and generate barcodes entirely offline.

OCR Text Recognition

Point your camera at a SAN sticker and the Tesseract.js engine reads the serial number. Works with printed labels, handwritten numbers, and low-contrast stickers. Camera or image upload.

AI Box Counting

SAN Registry

Offline-First PWA

Install on your phone and work without internet. Service worker caches the entire app, ML models, and OCR language data. Workbox runtime caching ensures models stay fresh. First visit warms the cache — every visit after that is instant.
